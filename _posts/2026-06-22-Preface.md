The idea of the blog was created in the not-that-far-away 2020, at the peak of
COVID-19 in Ukraine. I was thinking of a place to store some of my thoughts on
past situations and ideas in my head. Just for self-reflection. I noticed that
if I had something typed down, I could not only refer to the situation, but also
to the way I was thinking about it.

I wanted to make it publicly available so I'll be honest with my thoughts and
experiences. It may also be a reference for others who are interested in
described topics. You may even find something wrong and just prove me wrong. A
lot has happened since then. And I will gradually reveal details about some of
the things.
