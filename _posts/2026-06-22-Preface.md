The idea of the blog was created in not-that-far-away 2020, in the peak of COVID-19 in Ukraine. I was thinking on a place to store some of my thoughts for past situations and ideas in my head. Just for self-reflection. I noticed if I have something is typed down it will there, so I can not only refer to the situtation, but also to the way I was thinking about it.

I wanted to make it publicly available so I'll be honest with my thoughts and experiences. It may also be a reference for others who are interested in described topics. Or you may find something wrong and just prove it. Since then a lot of happened. And I will gradually reveal details about some of the things.
